Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Encyclopedia of Occultism" (Grimoire Archivum) application (the "App") handles your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled safely and responsibly.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, transmit, distribute, or sell your personal data.

The App is designed with your privacy in mind. All your personal data, including saved rituals, favorites, and daily practices, is stored locally on your device using Apple's native SwiftData framework. We do not maintain any external servers that receive or store your personal information.

2. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

The App offers Premium content unlocked via In-App Purchases and Subscriptions. All payment processing is securely handled directly by Apple through StoreKit. We do not collect, process, or have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID credentials.

Your purchase history and active subscription status are synchronized securely via your personal Apple ID to grant you access to Premium features.

3. Third-Party Services

Since the App operates offline and stores data locally, it does not integrate with third-party analytics trackers, advertising networks, or data-harvesting SDKs. Your app usage remains completely private.

4. Data Retention

Because your data is stored exclusively on your device, you maintain full control over it. You can delete all your data at any time by simply deleting the App from your device.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
